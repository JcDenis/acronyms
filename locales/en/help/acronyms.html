Activation

Widget "Acronyms" is available in the "Presentation widgets" menu only when the Enable public page of acronyms is checked.

Utilisation

Only acronyms added to this page "Manager acronyms" are listed in the public page of acronyms.
The acronyms in this list are automatically recognized by the system when using the wiki syntax. You just have to enter the acronym and the frame of double question marks regardless of its wording.

Example: ??ANSI??

Acronyms Acronym added through the button toolbar writing tickets are not automatically added to this list.

Note: to remove an acronym, just empty its entitled.
